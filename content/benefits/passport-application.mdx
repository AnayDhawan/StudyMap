# Applying for an Indian passport

> Placeholder guide. Always confirm the latest process and document list on the official Passport Seva portal.

A passport is worth getting early, well before you need it for an exam abroad or a trip. Here is the rough shape of the process.

## What you usually need

- Proof of identity and address (Aadhaar is commonly accepted).
- Proof of date of birth (birth certificate or school certificate).
- For minors, parents' documents and consent.

## The steps

1. Register on the official Passport Seva portal and create an account.
2. Fill the application form and pay the fee online.
3. Book an appointment at a Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Post Office PSK. You can find PSK locations on the **Map** under "passport office".
4. Visit the centre with your original documents and a printout of the application receipt.
5. Track the status online; police verification may follow before dispatch.

## Tips for students

- Apply during a holiday so an appointment does not clash with school.
- Use the **Map** to find the nearest passport office and get directions.
- Double-check that your name matches exactly across all documents.
