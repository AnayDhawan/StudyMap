# Get software free or discounted as a student

> Placeholder guide. Replace the links and details with verified, current offers before launch.

Most of the tools you need for school and side projects are free for students. The trick is proving you are one, usually with a school email address or a photo of your student ID.

## Start with the GitHub Student Developer Pack

The single best starting point. One application unlocks dozens of offers.

- Apply at the GitHub Education site with your school email or ID.
- Once approved, you get credits and free tiers across many partner tools.

## Common offers worth claiming

- **JetBrains**: free professional IDEs (IntelliJ, PyCharm, and more) with a student license.
- **Notion**: free Plus plan features for students and educators.
- **Figma**: free Education plan for design and prototyping.
- **Microsoft and Google**: many schools provide free Office or Workspace accounts; ask your school admin.

## Tips

- Use your school email wherever possible; it is the fastest proof.
- Keep a clear photo of your student ID for services that need manual review.
- Re-verify each year. Most student offers expire and need renewal.
